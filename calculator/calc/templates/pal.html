What to do with PAL?

PAL (Physical Activity Level) helps determine your daily activity level. Now that you know your PAL, you can use the CPM Calculator to estimate your Total Metabolic Rate (TMR)—the number of calories you need daily to maintain your weight.

This is useful if you want to lose weight: simply reduce your calorie intake below your TMR. For example, subtracting 500 kcal per day could help you lose around 0.5 kg per week.
